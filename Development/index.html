Development

If you are interested in how the ViTables development is going on you can visit its project site. From there you can:

Note

Feature Requests, Comments and Bug Reports

Your feedback is important! I'd really like to hear what you are doing with ViTables. It is the best way to adapt it to your needs. If you have feature requests, comments, kudos or you've found a bug, please send a message to the ViTables Users Group.
